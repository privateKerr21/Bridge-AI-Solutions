What custom AI-powered software looks like in practice

These are real tools and platforms built for small B2B service businesses — the kind of internal software that replaces the spreadsheets and manual steps eating your team's time.

Custom software applies across industries. These are examples of what it can look like — your specific bottleneck is the real starting point.

Consulting

High-margin work gets buried under proposal writing, meeting notes, and status updates. A custom platform handles the admin so your team stays billable.

Proposal Builder

A custom tool that generates first-draft proposals from discovery notes and a scoped template. Consistent formatting, faster turnaround, every engagement.

~3 hrs saved / week
Meeting Intelligence Tool

An internal tool that transcribes client calls and surfaces structured action items. Your team leaves every meeting with a clean record — no manual note-taking.

~2 hrs saved / week
Client Research Dashboard

A custom briefing platform that compiles company background, recent news, and talking points before every meeting. Show up prepared without spending an hour on research.

Client Reporting Platform

A purpose-built tool that compiles project updates into a formatted weekly report automatically. Clients stay informed; your team stays focused on delivery.
