What AI automation looks like in practice

These are real automation workflows built for professional services firms — the kind of work that eats hours every week and doesn't have to.

AI automation applies across industries. These are examples of what it can look like — your workflow is the real starting point.

Consulting

High-margin work gets buried under proposal writing, meeting notes, and status updates. Automation handles the admin so your team stays billable.

Proposal Drafting

Auto-generate first-draft proposals from a template and discovery notes. Cuts prep time and keeps formatting consistent across every engagement.

~3 hrs saved / week
Meeting Summaries

Transcribe and summarize client calls into structured action items automatically. No more manual note-taking after every call.

~2 hrs saved / week
Client Research Briefs

Pull together company background, recent news, and talking points before meetings. Show up prepared without spending an hour on manual research.

~2 hrs saved / week
Status Reports

Auto-compile project updates from your tools into a formatted weekly report. Clients stay informed; your team stays focused on delivery.

~1.5 hrs saved / week

Accounting

Data entry, document chasing, and recurring reports are the biggest time sinks in accounting. Automation handles the repetition so you can focus on advisory work.

Invoice Processing

Extract line items from incoming invoices and log them to your system automatically. Reduces manual data entry and entry errors at scale.

~4 hrs saved / week
Client Onboarding Docs

Generate engagement letters, checklists, and intake forms from a client profile. First impressions are consistent, professional, and fast.

~2 hrs saved / week
Tax Document Collection

Send automated reminders and track document receipt per client. Nothing falls through the cracks during busy season.

~3 hrs saved / week
Recurring Reports

Auto-generate monthly or quarterly financial summaries from your data. Reports go out on time without manual assembly every cycle.

~2 hrs saved / week

Marketing Agencies

Client reporting, content reformatting, and lead qualification drain creative bandwidth. Automation takes the grind work off your team's plate.

Content Repurposing

Turn a single blog post or video into social copy, email snippets, and ad variations automatically. One asset, five formats, minimal effort.

~3 hrs saved / week
Client Reporting

Pull campaign metrics and generate formatted client reports automatically. Looks polished without the manual spreadsheet assembly.

~2 hrs saved / week
Lead Qualification

Score and triage inbound leads based on fit criteria before they hit your CRM. Your sales team focuses only on the right conversations.

~2 hrs saved / week
Creative Brief Generation

Convert intake form answers into a structured creative brief automatically. Briefings are consistent and kickoffs start faster.

~1.5 hrs saved / week

Recruitment

Screening, outreach, and scheduling eat hours that should go toward building relationships. Automation handles the coordination so recruiters stay in conversations.

Candidate Screening

Summarize resumes against job requirements and flag top matches automatically. First-pass review in minutes, not hours.

~4 hrs saved / week
Outreach Sequences

Draft personalized outreach messages from a candidate profile and role description. Higher response rates, less time per message.

~2 hrs saved / week
Job Description Writing

Generate first-draft JDs from a role intake form. Consistent quality and tone without starting from a blank page every time.

~1.5 hrs saved / week
Interview Scheduling

Automate scheduling coordination between candidates and hiring managers. Eliminates the back-and-forth email chain entirely.

~1 hr saved / week

Construction

Job site admin, bidding, and compliance paperwork pile up fast. Automation handles the back-office load so your crews stay focused on the build.

Daily Site Reports

Auto-generate end-of-day reports from crew notes and site updates. Keeps clients informed and creates a paper trail without the admin overhead.

~3 hrs saved / week
Estimate & Quote Generation

Draft project estimates from a scope of work template and materials list. Faster turnaround on bids means more jobs won.

~3 hrs saved / week
Subcontractor Coordination

Automate scheduling reminders, document requests, and status follow-ups with subs. Less chasing, more building.

~2 hrs saved / week
Safety & Compliance Docs

Auto-generate toolbox talk summaries, incident reports, and inspection checklists. Stay compliant without the paperwork pile.

~2 hrs saved / week
