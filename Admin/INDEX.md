## Purpose
Business administration files. Keep financial and legal records organized and accessible.

## What Goes Where

### Invoices/
- Sent invoices (organized by client/year)
- Payment receipts
- Expense records
- Financial reports

### Contracts/
- Signed client agreements
- Vendor contracts
- Partnership agreements
- Amendments and addendums

### Legal/
- NDAs (signed)
- Terms of service
- Privacy policies
- Business registration documents
- Insurance documents

## Naming Convention
- Invoices: `[YYYY-MM-DD]_[Client]_Invoice-[Number].pdf`
- Contracts: `[YYYY-MM-DD]_[Client]_[Contract Type].pdf`

## Record Keeping
- Keep copies of all signed documents
- Organize by year for easy retrieval
- Back up regularly
- Note renewal dates for ongoing contracts
