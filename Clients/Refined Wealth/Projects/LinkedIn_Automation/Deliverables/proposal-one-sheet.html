The Challenge You're Facing

Manually logging connection dates, message dates, and response status in spreadsheets that feel like they're "at the bottom of the totem pole."

Missed Follow-Up Timing

No automated reminders means guesswork and missed timing windows. "People followed up on XYZ day should have been followed up with today."

Lost Opportunities

"There's missed opportunity by inactivity happening." When follow-ups don't happen, warm prospects go cold and revenue is lost.

The Real Cost: It's not just the 8-10 hours per week. It's the mental overhead of remembering who to reach out to, the stress of knowing people are being forgotten, and the revenue lost from prospects who never got that critical 2nd or 3rd touch.
How We Solve This

We're not offering a generic CRM. This is a custom follow-up automation system built specifically for your LinkedIn workflow, designed to eliminate the tracking burden while keeping you in complete control.

1

Smart Follow-Up Sequencing

The system tells you exactly who needs follow-up today:

  • 3 days after 1st message (no response)
  • 5 days after 2nd message (no response)
  • 9 days after 3rd message (no response)
  • 15 days after 4th message (no response)
  • Monthly thereafter (eternal nurture)
2

Fully Automated via Expandi

Set it and forget it — Expandi runs your follow-ups automatically:

  • Messages send on schedule — no manual work
  • Automatic reply detection stops sequences
  • No spreadsheets, no tracking, no data entry
  • You focus on conversations, not administration
3

Two-Track System

Separate handling for different engagement levels:

  • "No Response" — Automated 3→5→9→15→monthly
  • "Active Engagement" — Custom follow-up for replies
  • Easy to see who's engaged vs. who needs nurturing
  • Zero prospects fall through the cracks
4

Real-Time Dashboard

See exactly what's happening across all your outreach:

  • Live view of who's been contacted and when
  • Reply notifications — never miss a response
  • Performance metrics and response rates
  • Full visibility without digging through spreadsheets
