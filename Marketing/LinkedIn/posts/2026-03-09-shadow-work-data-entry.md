**Date**: 2026-03-09
**Status**: Approved, ready to post
**Theme**: Relatable scene → reframe cost → introduce shadow work concept → soft CTA

---

It's 5pm on a Friday.

Good week. Real conversations. A few new leads came in.

Now you're copying them from your inbox into a spreadsheet. One by one.

Name. Email. Company. What they asked about.

45 minutes. Same as last Friday.

---

You've probably done this routine for years.

If it's once a week, that's 39 hours a year spent moving information that already existed — from one place to another.

Not building. Not selling. Copy. Paste. Repeat.

---

I call this "shadow work."

It's the invisible labor that keeps a business running but doesn't actually grow it. Not dramatic enough to feel like a real problem. Just... always there.

The thing is — this is exactly what AI automation is built for.

Not the big sci-fi stuff. Just: when a lead comes in, the data is already where it needs to be. No Friday ritual required.
