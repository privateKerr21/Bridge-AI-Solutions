## Purpose
Central repository for all Rocket AI brand assets and media files.

## What Goes Where

### Logos/
- Primary logo (PNG, SVG, PDF)
- Logo variations (dark, light, icon-only)
- Favicon and app icons
- Social media profile images

### Brand/
- Brand guidelines document
- Color palette (hex codes, RGB, CMYK)
- Typography specifications
- Voice and tone guidelines

### Media/
- Stock photos (licensed)
- Custom graphics
- Video assets
- Social media graphics
- Marketing collateral

## Asset Guidelines
- Keep high-resolution originals
- Name files descriptively: `rocket-ai-logo-dark-500px.png`
- Organize by type and purpose
- Track licenses for purchased assets
